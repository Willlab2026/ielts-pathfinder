Activity 1

Chapter 2 — Understanding the Four-Part Structure

Read the Task

Look at the IELTS Task 1 question carefully. Then answer the questions below to identify the basic information in the task. These details will help you understand what the report should be about.

WRITING TASK 1

You should spend about 20 minutes on this task.

The table below shows the proportion of different categories of families living in poverty in Australia in 1999.

Summarise the information by selecting and reporting the main features, and make comparisons where relevant.

Write at least 150 words.

Questions

1 What type of visual is used in this task?
A. A line graph
B. A bar chart
C. A table
D. A pie chart
2 Which country is the information about?
A. Australia
B. The United Kingdom
C. Canada
D. New Zealand
3 What is the time period?
A. 1989
B. 1999
C. 2009
D. 2019
4 What unit is mainly used in the table?
A. Dollars
B. Percentages
C. Kilometres
D. Hours
5 What kinds of groups are compared in the table?
A. Different age groups
B. Different income groups
C. Different family or household types
D. Different Australian cities
6 What does the table show?
A. The number of poor families in different countries
B. The proportion of different family types living in poverty in Australia
C. The income of different household types in Australia
D. The percentage of children living in poverty in Australia
7 Which sentence below best summarises the task?
A. The table shows poverty in Australia.
B. The table shows the proportion of people from different household types living in poverty in Australia in 1999.
C. The table compares poor people and rich people in Australia in 1999.
D. The table shows the number of families in Australia.
Have a Think

Where do you think this sentence should appear in a Task 1 report?

"The table shows the proportion of people from different household types living in poverty in Australia in 1999."

Keep this question in mind. You will discover the answer in the next activity.
